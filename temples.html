Srivaishnava Temples in Telangana

Sri Ranganathaswamy Temple, Jiyaguda (Hyderabad)

400‑year‑old Vaishnava temple by the Musi river, built by Sri Nanganur Prathama Peetham; follows Sri Vaishnava tradition.

Yadadri Lakshmi Narasimha Swamy Temple

Pancha‑Narasimha shrine following the Tenkalai Sri Vaishnava tradition in Telangana.

Sri Sita Ramachandra Swamy Temple, Bhadrachalam

Major Divya Desam shrine in Telangana and key Sri Vaishnava deity centre by the Godavari.

Gudem Gutta Satyanarayana Swamy Temple (Mancherial)

Hill‑top Satyanarayana Swamy temple on the banks of Godavari, called “Telangana’s Annavaram”.

Anantha Padmanabha Swamy Temple, Ananthagiri (Vikarabad)

Lord Vishnu temple in scenic Ananthagiri hills, established in Sri Vaishnava tradition.

Dharmapuri Sri Lakshmi Narasimha Temple (Jagtial)

Historic Narasimha temple on Godavari bank known as “Dakshina Kashi”.

Vemulawada Rajarajeshwara Temple (Karimnagar)

Shiva temple but revered by Sri Vaishnavas as Abhimana sthalam in Telangana.

Sri Veeranarayana Swamy Temple, Kolanupaka (Warangal)

Vishnu shrine dating to 403 AD, significant Vaishnava heritage site near Warangal.

Alampur Navabrahma / Jogulamba Temple

A historic Vaishnava‑Shaiva cluster and also a Shakti Peeth in Jogulamba Gadwal.

Sri Vidya Saraswati Temple, Wargal (Siddipet)

Prominent Sri Vaishnava Saraswati shrine, especially for Aksharabhyasam ceremonies.
